About

Initiation

Ideation

This project began as an idea born from my passion for knowledge — a platform where people can both learn and participate. While there are already other options available, I wanted to create my own design and develop a unique experience. It’s something I’ve been continuously improving

First Project

Digital Magazine

I first envisioned a digital magazine dedicated to articles that share research and knowledge, with the goal of eventually opening it up for contributions from a wider community

Second Project

Statistics

My vision was to build a statistics website where data comes alive through interactive visuals. By showing it on a map, I aimed to make the information not only informative but also entertaining and easy to explore

Third Project

Documentaries Films

My third idea was to build a platform dedicated to short documentary films. Over time, I wanted to invite users to contribute, with the vision of growing it into a much larger project
